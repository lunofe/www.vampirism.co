Join The Team

We’re always grateful for calm, fair, and reliable people who care about the community and want to help keep the server a safe and enjoyable place for everyone.

Becoming staff is not a perk, a status symbol, or a fast track to power. It’s a responsibility. As a staff member, you represent the server at all times - your actions, tone, and decisions directly impact players’ experiences and the health of the community.

Staff members are expected to:

  • Treat everyone fairly and respectfully, regardless of personal opinions
  • Stay calm and objective, even in heated situations
  • Help players patiently and empathetically
  • Follow procedures, document actions, and work as part of a team
  • Accept feedback, learn from mistakes, and improve over time
