Frequently Asked Questions

  1. How can I join the server?

    First, get one of our modpacks. You should find a button in the main menu that allows you to join our server.

  2. Can I play with a cracked/pirated version of Minecraft?

    No. You need a genuine Minecraft account that you’ve paid money for.

  3. Does the server do resets/wipes?

    We perform a full server wipe every New Year’s Day. Previous worlds are archived and made available for players to download and use locally.

    The Nether and The End receive an additional wipe in mid-summer.

  4. Can I use Optifine?

    Our modpack already includes open-source alternatives that offer better performance and nearly identical features.

    Because of this, there’s little reason to use OptiFine, and we don’t provide support for it.

  5. How do I report a player or submit a complaint?

    If there are staff members on the server, talk to them directly.

    Alternatively, open a ticket in the support channel of our Discord.
