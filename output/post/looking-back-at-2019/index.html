Looking back at 2019

Hey folks,

first of all, I wish everyone a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year - which brings us directly to the topic.

The people who have been here a bit longer probably already know, that the server will reset around New Year's Eve. It may take us a few more days to complete everything on our end, we’ll keep you updated throughout the process.

Vampirism for 1.14 is still in it's alpha, Forge isn’t completed nor stable either and Sponge's development is not really fast as well, since they need to wait for Forge and then update their own stuff. Not to forget all the plugins we'd need… Once Vampirism for 1.14 is finished, or at least in a state that is playable, we’ll check if it’s the same for Forge and Sponge and then think about upgrading to 1.14.

We originally planned to keep the current seed, but after thinking about it, we decided to change it, since a new world will mean a new world for you to explore.
