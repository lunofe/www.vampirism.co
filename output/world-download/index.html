World Download

We perform a full server wipe every New Year’s Day. Previous worlds are archived and made available for players to download and use locally.

We do this for a couple of reasons:

  • Resource Exhaustion: Due to technical limitations, the world of our server isn’t infinite and therefore has limited resources, that would eventually disappear from existence.
  • Abandonment: As a public server dedicated to more or less one topic, it’s clear that new players will come and old players will go. However, old players leave behind claims, that can’t be used by other, new players.
  • Interest: As said before, this server is about more or less one topic and it’s less likely you’ll see the next hot minigame here that’ll keep you playing for another thousand hours. Minecraft just get’s boring after some time. So why not start over?
